ACME CORP — EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
For New Hires | Updated January 2025

=== BEFORE YOUR FIRST DAY ===

Within 48 hours of receiving your offer letter:
1. Complete all paperwork in Workday (link in your welcome email): I-9, direct deposit, tax forms, benefits enrollment
2. Order your equipment if you're remote (link in the welcome email — choose by Day -3)
3. Set up your @acmecorp.com email via the link in your onboarding email
4. Download Slack and join with your company email

=== WEEK 1: ORIENTATION ===

Day 1:
- 9:00 AM: Virtual welcome call with your manager
- 10:00 AM: IT setup session (confirm your equipment is working)
- 11:00 AM: Company orientation with HR (Zoom link in calendar invite)
- 2:00 PM: Team introduction call
- Rest of day: Read through this guide, explore tools

Day 2–3:
- Complete mandatory training modules in the Learning Management System (lms.acmecorp.internal):
  * Security Awareness Training (required within 3 days)
  * Harassment Prevention Training (required within 7 days)
  * Company Values and Culture session
- Shadow a team member on their daily workflow
- Set up your Slack profile (add photo, pronouns, timezone, role)

Day 4–5:
- Begin your 30-Day Onboarding Plan (shared by your manager in Notion)
- Meet with your onboarding buddy (assigned by HR)
- Attend your first team standup

=== YOUR 30/60/90 DAY PLAN ===

30 Days — Learn:
- Understand your team's goals and current projects
- Complete all onboarding training
- Meet with each direct team member 1:1
- Shadow at least 5 customer calls (if customer-facing)
- Understand the product inside and out

60 Days — Contribute:
- Begin taking ownership of small tasks or projects
- Identify 1–2 areas where you can make an immediate impact
- Share your observations and fresh perspective in team retros
- Complete your first performance check-in with your manager

90 Days — Own:
- Drive at least one project independently
- Build relationships across teams
- Set goals for the next quarter with your manager
- Complete your 90-day self-review in Workday

=== PAYROLL ===

Payroll runs bi-weekly on Fridays. The cutoff for changes (address, direct deposit, etc.) is the Wednesday before payday.

Your first paycheck may be prorated based on your start date. Payslips are available in Workday under Pay > Pay History.

Questions about your paycheck: payroll@acmecorp.com or Slack #payroll-questions (private, HR-monitored).

=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q: When do I get my company credit card?
A: Credit cards are issued at 6 months of tenure, for employees with a business need. Request through your manager.

Q: How do I request a standing desk or other equipment?
A: Submit an equipment request in the IT portal. Budget approvals require manager sign-off for items over $500.

Q: What's the dress code?
A: Business casual for client-facing meetings. Smart casual or comfortable for regular days. Use good judgment.

Q: I made a mistake. What should I do?
A: Tell your manager as soon as possible. We value transparency over perfection. Mistakes are learning opportunities.

Q: Can I work from a coffee shop or co-working space?
A: Yes, with VPN connected. For co-working space reimbursement (up to $200/month), submit receipts to expenses with code "CO-WORK."
